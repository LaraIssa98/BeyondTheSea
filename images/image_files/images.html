Manama - A city that is known for its old and charming pearl-diving Gulf identity.

Offering various entertainment, dining and shopping outlets, Manama is the perfect family-friendly destination for those looking for a weekend escape. When it comes to entertainment, Manama offers thrilling activities for adrenaline junkies such as drag racing and skydiving; great dining options for picky foodies that include casual and gourmet; as well as unlimited options for bargain hunters and luxury shoppers.

Popular Attractions

Enjoy our top recommended attractions for your upcoming trip!

City Centre Bahrain

With an indoor-outdoor waterpark, a 20-screen cinema and over 60 dining outlets, City Centre Bahrain is one of the city’s top shopping destinations. The mall also features an international mix of over 340 shops and boutique stores that include high-end brands such as Saks Fifth Avenue and Michael Kors, as well as high-street labels such as New Look and Forever 21.

Amwaj Islands

This collection of man-made islands can be found near the coast of Muharraq island in the Persian Gulf. Amwaj Islands comprise of six-themed islands: Tala Island, Najmah, Dalphene, Hamama, Farasha Island, and Wardeh. Guests can explore various facilities such as designated business areas, cafes and restaurants, villas with private beaches, shopping malls and a water park.

Lost Paradise of Dilmun water park

With a variety of restaurants and a sandy beach, the Lost Paradise of Dilmun water park is the place to be if you’re looking to spend a fun day with the family. Featuring fun attractions such as the Falcon’s Dive, the Speed Slides and the Tylosplash, this water park is guaranteed to offer a memorable experience for the whole family.

Top Restaurants

Dine in style at one of these iconic restaurants!

Abd El Wahab Restaurant

This Lebanese eatery offers a relaxed atmosphere suitable for a family outing. Located at Moda Mall, Abd El Wahab restaurant is best known for providing mouth-watering grills and delicious starters.

Cafe Lilou

With its stylish decor and succulent French cuisine, Cafe Lilou is a little piece of France in the heart of Bahrain. The cafe is a popular hit with both locals and tourists and is famous for its tasty desserts.

The Cheesecake Factory

With over 200 branches around the world, the Cheesecake Factory is one of Bahrain's top family-friendly dining establishments. The restaurant offers over 250 menu items and an impressive collection of more than 50 signature cheesecakes and desserts.

Top Events

Visit Bahrain & enjoy fun family-friendly events!

Bahrain Summer Festival

Considered one of the country's most anticipated annual events, the Bahrain Summer Festival is taking place at Nakhool Tent at the historical Arad Fort from 18 June to 18 August 2018. The festival offers various family-friendly activities as well as entertaining live shows and creative workshops.

FAI World Cup of Indoor Skydiving 2018 Bahrain

Taking place at Gravity Bahrain from 25th to 28th October 2018, the 3rd FAI World Cup of Indoor Skydiving is an event no adrenaline junkie should miss!

Jewellery Arabia 2018

The Middle East International Jewellery & Watch Exhibition is the Middle East’s largest and most prestigious jewellery exhibition. Shop stunning jewellery, watches and accessories from 20th to 24th November 2018 at the Bahrain International Exhibition & Convention Centre.
